The Future Of GAMING Is Here

The next generation of games is here, releasing the newest consoles, from the PS5 and Xbox Series X to the Oculus Quest 2. They're bringing into play factors that have been influencing every new generation of games: graphics, processing power, and online connectivity. This year is no different, except that all three are being revolutionised in unprecedented ways.Graphics have always been a big part of what makes a console a generational leap. The PS3 brought us high-definition graphics; the PS4 Pro brought us 4K resolution. The PS5 will bring us ray tracing - a technology that has made its way into gaming from movie production, where it's used to create effects like glow and reflections in real-time. It means incredibly realistic lighting effects that give you more immersion than ever before.
